Gearhead 1b, 3b Model

Built upon Llama 3.2 1b and 3b, this custom model is focused on mobile equipment diagnostics and troubleshooting. I've finetuned the 1b model with over 4000 diagnostic conversation examples and am working to compile a vast database of service and technical manuals for a comprehensive RAG implementation. The goal of the Gearhead project is to have a mobile device deployable AI assistant to help backfill skillgaps for service technicians.
